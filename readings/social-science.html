Crowdsourcing and Human Computation

Readings for Topic 7 : Crowdsourcing and social science

Assigned TBA

  • "The Wisdom of Crowds" Chapter 11 - Markets
  • Citizen Science: Can Volunteers Do Real Research?, discussion of the ability of non-experts to collect scientific data.
  • Mechanical Turk is not Anonymous, discusses a privacy vunerability in MTurk, and the implications for research ethics.
  • Dirty Deeds Done Dirt Cheap, discussion of some of the potentially malicious uses of crowdsourcing. Mostly included for the fantastically scathing title.
