Crowdsourcing and Human Computation

Readings for Topic 6 : Applications to human computer interaction (HCI) design

Assigned TBA

  • "The Wisdom of Crowds" Chapter 6 - Society Does Exist
  • Soylent : A Word Processor with a Crowd Inside, a plugin for MS Word which allows users to have their work edited by Turkers.
  • VizWiz: Nearly Real-time Answers to Visual Questions, an app which uses MTurk to help blind people with simple tasks, like finding matching socks or reading the nutrition labels.
  • Games with a purpose, clever ideas for tricking people into having fun so you don't have to pay them to label your data.
